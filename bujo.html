WHAT IS A BULLET JOURNAL?

An Explanation

A bullet journal is a method of organization. The classic system helps organize schedules, lists, reminders, and tasks in one notebook. There are many articles and books about the "classic" way to bullet journal, but the way I describe it is a planner system that is customizable and allows me to have creative power over my organizational system.
