13 Years as a Suzuki Piano Student

MY JOURNEY

From 9th grade on...

Piano has been apart of my life ever since I was 6 years old, making this my 11th year of learning the piano. I learn the Suzuki style of piano, which encompasses the teachings of listening to the music and repetition, and believes in the power of using music to develop good moral character. I learn mostly classical music. My favorite composer of all time is Frederic Chopin (I have now played three of his pieces), but my favorite piece is Tchaikovsky's Piano Concerto No. 1 in B-Flat Minor. I am reaching my final graduation in piano- I will graduate from Book 10 in February of 2019. Although this will be my last level in piano, I will take this art with me the rest of my life and I hope to continue this in college.
